Prediction Height Application

Predict child's height from parent's height

David Ib치침ez Alonso

Motivation and App Explanation

  • We want to create an application to predict the child's height knowing the parent's height.
  • We are using the data from the famous 1885 study of Francis Galton, exploring the relationship between the heights of children and the height of their parents, to build our predicting model. The variables are the height of the adult child and the midparent height, defined as the average of the height of the father and 1.08 times the height of the mother. The units are inches. The number of cases is 928, representing 928 children and their 205 parents.
  • As input we have the Parent's Height.
  • Inputs height work from 50 to 90 inches.
  • As output we have the predicted Child's Height.
  • We have also a plot with our prediction, galton's data and the linear model we build.
  • You can find the Applicatio here https://pinap.shinyapps.io/DDP_Project/
